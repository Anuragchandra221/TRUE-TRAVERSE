THE POWER BANK

DESCRIPTION

This is a multipurpose Solar Power Bank with inbuilt Lithium Battery & Solar Panel which makes it a plug & play solar kit as well. It comes with an inbuilt LED light & 3 lithium battery of 3.7V-2000mAh which makes it the perfect portable solar kit suitable smart phone mobile charging which is supported by a universal female USB port. The inbuilt lithium battery can recharge almost all smartphones.The solar panel can charge the battery within 6 hours which can also be charged via electricity making the system hybrid as well.
